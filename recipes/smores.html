Recipe for making S'Mores

Description

A classic meal for campfires, or any other occasion of your liking.

It is very simple to make all you need is three ingredients and a fire.

Ingredients

  • Large graham cracker
  • Large marshmellow
  • Hershey's chocolate bar

Steps

    e
  1. Make sure a controlled fire is going.
  2. Split graham cracker into two pieces.
  3. Put the marshmellow on a stick and cook it in the fire.
  4. Remove the marshmellow from the fire after it is cooked.
  5. Put the marshmellow on top of one piece of the graham cracker.
  6. Then take a piece of the chocolate bar and put it on top of the marshmellow.
  7. Finally, put the other piece of the graham cracker on top of the chocolate.
  8. Enjoy your meal.
